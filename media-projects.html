Media Projects

This section highlights creative and visual projects related to storytelling, design, editing, and interactive media. These projects reflect my interest in combining creativity with structured thinking and polished visual presentation.

Charming Labyrinth

Charming Labyrinth is a level design project created for the game Terrorarium. The project focuses on designing a maze-like environment that guides the player through exploration, pacing, and challenge while maintaining a strong visual identity.

This project reflects my ability to plan structured environments, think about user experience, and refine a design through iteration and testing.

Punk's Not Dead

Punk's Not Dead is a digital visual project created for a contest by the band La Vida Boheme for their music video Control. The project focused on visual composition, editing, and storytelling.

It reflects my interest in combining creative direction with structured design and visual storytelling.
